Edge AI CNNs VLMs Wearables

AI-Powered Vegetation Risk Management

Led product development at Deloitte for a large US utility to automate the inspection of thousands of miles of power infrastructure using drones, LiDAR, and machine learning. The system detects at-risk vegetation encroachments near high-voltage lines — the single largest cause of service outages per FERC — replacing costly on-foot inspections with scalable aerial remote sensing. Integrated GIS data layers, Salesforce Field Service Cloud, and ML image processing into a unified platform for real-time reporting and work order management.

Computer Vision Drone / UAS LiDAR GIS / Geospatial Salesforce

US Power Plants & Facility GHG Emissions

Built interactive Tableau visualizations mapping the full US energy infrastructure — spanning power plants by energy source and total capacity, alongside facility-level GHG emissions inventories. A refined version of this work was delivered to state leadership to help policymakers understand and decarbonize the energy systems they oversee.

Tableau Geospatial Analytics Energy Policy GHG / Emissions

Inline Solid-Liquid Mixing Device

Two-semester capstone project at Rice University to design and prototype an inline device capable of continuously mixing one solid and two liquids for large-scale chemical production. The system integrates an auger-fed hopper, swirl basin, pumps, and real-time sensors for salinity, flow, and density — all controlled via a Raspberry Pi with touchscreen interface for user input and QC reporting.

CAD / SolidWorks Electrical Design Sensors & Control Raspberry Pi

Mechanical Design: 80:1 Gearbox Assembly

Designed a compact gearbox achieving an 80:1 speed reduction with input/output shafts at 90° and 12,000 lb-in output torque. Optimized the geartrain for minimum volume using four spur gear pairs and one bevel gear set across six shafts. Performed full fatigue and stress analyses for shaft and bearing selection, achieving safety factors of 2.1:1 (first cycle) and 1.55:1 (infinite life). Received a perfect score of 225/225.

CAD / SolidWorks Fatigue Analysis Gear Train Design FEA
